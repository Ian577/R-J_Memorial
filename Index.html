Romeo

Romeo Montague was the beloved cousin to Benvolio Montague, and the best friend to Mercutio. They did everything together, including sneaking into the Capulets party, where Romeo, met Juliet.

Juliet Capulet

Juliet Capulet was the young daughter to lord Capulet, and cousin to Tybalt. She met Romeo at her family's party, the same night her parents told her they wanted her to mary Count Paris.

Their Marriage

Romeo and Juliet wed in secret just a day after they met at the Capulets party. Friar Lawrence preformed the ceremony. Only an hour later did Romeo and Tybalt have their fatal duel, whcih led to Romeo's banishment.
